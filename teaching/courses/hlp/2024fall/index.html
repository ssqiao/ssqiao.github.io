Grading Policy

  • \({G}_{OutClass}\): Programming Score Out of Class„ÄêOpened within Each Week„Äë Reference Allowed but No Plagiarism
  • \({G}_{InClass}\): Programming Score In Class„ÄêOpened on Experimental Sessions„ÄëReference and Plagiarism Not Allowed

Award Policy

Outstanding Performance \({G}_{award}=3/time\) (Cumulative), including:

  • Topic Discussion
  • Experience Share
  • etc.

Penalty Policy

Bad Behavior \({G}_{penalty}=5/time\) (Cumulative), including but not limited to

  • In Class: playing phone, whispering, doing homework, etc.
  • Online Platforms: spamming or flooding discussions, unhealthy or inappropriate remarks, spreading negativity or pessimism, etc.

Absence Policy

Attendance Penalty \({G}_{absence}\)

  • One time Without Reason: \({G}_{absence}=10\)
  • Two times Without Reason: \({G}_{absence}=30\)
  • Three and More Without Reason: \({G}_{absence}=60\)
